RECALLED HAPPINESS: A EUPHORIC AND NOSTALGIC JOURNEY TO REMEMBER

As the academic year comes to an end, the Class of 2023–2024 graduates are experiencing a flood of mixed emotions, including nostalgia and excitement. When they think back on the four years that have passed, memories flash through their heads like a compilation of special occasions. Every experience, from the anxious anticipation of orientation day to the exuberant celebrations of sporting triumphs, is ingrained in the collective mind of the graduating class. The friendship created via group projects, late-night study sessions propelled by coffee, and breaks filled with laughter that reverberate through the hallways are all integral parts of their shared experiences. But there were also difficult and challenging times amid the jokes and friendship. The sleepless nights spent studying for tests, the pressure of impending deadlines, and the sporadic failures that tried their fortitude have all aided in their personal development. Nevertheless, the graduating class is more resilient and stronger than before, despite all of the difficulties. They are both excited and nervous as they stand on the brink of a new chapter in their lives. For some, it's the chance to pursue a career path they've always desired. For others, it represents the chance to pursue new interests or intellectual endeavors. A tangible sense of appreciation and pride permeates the air as they get ready to say goodbye to the comfortable faces and familiar halls that have become their second home. Thank you for the memories made, the lessons learnt, and the friendships that were established. Take pride in their achievements and the fortitude they've shown in the face of difficulty. Emotions are running high in the days before graduation as they get ready to start the next phase of their lives. Excitement for the future permeates the air, slightly overshadowed by melancholy for the memories they leave behind. Above all, though, is a deep sense of appreciation for the path we've traveled together and the ties that will always bind us as members of the Class of 2023–2024.
